  1. Matching of your Finance Request with Investor Offers and Murabaha Contract
  1. Once your Finance Request has been matched with each Investor’s Offer and you have entered into this Agreement pursuant to Clause 5.10 of the Nester Service Terms (“Start Date”) and each Payment Date thereafter, the obligations in this Clause 1 will be performed.

  1. Commodity purchase by the Investor from the Selling Broker:

  1. Each Investor shall buy via the Service the Commodity from a third party commodity broker (“the Selling Broker”) on the Commodity Trading Platform under the Commodity Sale Terms for the spot price (“Cost Price”) on the basis that the legal title to the Commodity is received and held by Nester Security Trustee Ltd (“NST”) acting as trustee for and on behalf of the Investors under Clauses 2.2 and 2.3 of the Nester Service Terms (“the Commodity Purchase”);

  2. Nester will debit the Nester Account of each Investor for the Cost Price and record the transfer of legal title to the Commodity to NST (acting as trustee for and on behalf of the Investors) and the corresponding equitable title to the Commodity to each of the relevant Investors;

  1. Murabaha Sale by the Investor to the Buyer:

  1. immediately after the completion of the Commodity Purchase, the Investor shall sell the Investor’s right, title and interest in the Commodity to the Buyer via the Service under this Agreement for the Murabaha Price by making an offer to sell the Commodity via the Service including the following information; the description of the Commodity, the quantity, the Cost Price, the Profit and the Operating Fee (the total being the Murabaha Price) and the Murabaha Instalments and their Payment Dates on the condition that

immediately upon the occurrence of the Commodity On-Sale, the Buyer shall be obliged to pay to the Investor the Murabaha Price under this Agreement in the Murabaha Instalments via the Buyer’s Payment Account in the manner specified under Clause 2 of this Agreement and Clause 6.2 of the Nester Service Terms (the “Seller’s Offer”); and the Buyer shall immediately accept the Seller’s Offer via the Service (“the Murabaha Sale”);

  1. immediately upon the occurrence of the Murabaha Sale:

  1. Nester will record the transfer of each Investor’s right, title and interest in the Commodity to the Buyer;

  2. NST shall receive and hold the title to the Commodity as trustee for and on behalf of the Buyer under Clauses 2.2 and 2.3 of the Nester Service Terms;

  3. Nester will then record a debit in the Buyer’s Nester Account for the total amount of the Murabaha Price, pending payment of the Murabaha Instalments by the Buyer under this Agreement;

  1. The Commodity On-Sale by the Buyer to the Buying Broker:

  1. immediately after the sale under Clause 1.2(b)(ii) of this Agreement, NST as trustee for the Buyer shall sell the legal title to, and the Buyer’s right, title and interest in, the Commodity on the Commodity Trading Platform under the Commodity Sale Terms to a third party commodity broker (“the Buying Broker”) at the Cost Price (“the Commodity On-Sale”);

  2. Nester will then credit the Buyer’s Nester Account for the amount of the Cost Price less the Fees and any other deductions that may be specified under this Agreement;

  3. The price of the Commodity under the Commodity Purchase Agreement and the Commodity On-Sale Agreement at the Cost Price in Clauses 1.2(a) and 1.2(c) respectively shall be netted off by the Commodity Platform Operator, so that no funds actually need to be transferred between the Investor and the operator of the Commodity Trading Service.

  1. Other than agreeing to sell the Commodity to the Buying Broker under the Commodity On-Sale in accordance with Clause 1.2(c)(i), you must not try to sell the Commodity, assign or transfer the Commodity, or give the Commodity away, or use the Commodity as security or allow any other person to obtain any rights over the Commodity.
  2. In addition, you must:
  1. ensure no person has any rights in the Commodity as a result of anything you do, such as a right to recover the Commodity or a right to sell the Commodity, except the Buying Broker under the Commodity On-Sale Agreement;
  2. not sell, pledge, charge, assign, sub-let or otherwise dispose of the Commodity or attempt to do so, other than to the Buying Broker under the Commodity On-Sale;
  3. pay all licence fees, duties, charges, taxes and other amounts payable in relation to your purchase, ownership and on-sale of the Commodity.

  1. Payment
  1. You must repay the Outstanding Amount under each Murabaha Contract by paying the Murabaha Instalments on the Instalment Date shown in the Murabaha Contract (or, where you enter into more than one Murabaha Contract at or about the same time, the relevant Murabaha Contract Summary). Prompt payment is essential.
  2. Your obligation to pay the Murabaha Instalments will be satisfied by making those payments into your Payment Account (or such other payment account as Nester may approve in writing) in accordance with the Nester Service Terms.
  3. Upon receipt of each Murabaha Instalment in your Payment Account, Nester will credit the amount of each Murabaha Instalment to the Buyer’s Nester Account and immediately debit the Buyer’s Nester Account and credit each Investor’s Nester Account for the amount of the Murabaha Instalment that corresponds to the Murabaha Instalment owed to each Investor.
  4. Each Murabaha Instalment will be pro-rated between each of the Investors in accordance with each Murabaha Contract they have with the Buyer, and will be used:
  1. first to pay the amount of any arrears of Cost Price under the Murabaha Contract;
  2. then to pay the amount of Profit due under the latest monthly Murabaha Instalment that is due and payable under the Murabaha Contract;
  3. then to pay any fees, charges, costs or expenses due to Nester or any third party under the Murabaha Contract.
  1. All Murabaha Instalments will be paid to each of the Investors without any deduction (including but not limited to income tax) by the Buyer.  However, Nester will deduct the Operating Fee from each Murabaha Instalment for its own account.
